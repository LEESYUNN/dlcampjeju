Yerim Choi

Extra Form

Yerim Choi is currently an assistant professor of Industrial and Management Engineering at Kyonggi University. 
She was a data scientist at Naver Labs and received her B.S. and Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from Seoul National University. 
She tries to solve the real problems of mankind using data science and AI technologies.
